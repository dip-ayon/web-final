About Us

Preserving the legacy of Bangladesh's Liberation War

Our Mission

The Liberation War Museum Digital Archive is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich history of Bangladesh's struggle for independence. Our comprehensive collection includes artifacts, documents, photographs, and personal stories that tell the story of our nation's journey to freedom.

Our Collection

With over thousands of carefully curated items, our digital archive serves as a bridge between the past and future generations. From historical documents and personal diaries to weapons and memorabilia, each artifact tells a unique story of courage, sacrifice, and determination.

Accessibility

Our digital platform makes these important historical resources accessible to researchers, students, and the general public worldwide. Through advanced search capabilities and detailed documentation, we ensure that the legacy of our liberation war remains alive and accessible.
